The problem

Immigration case preparation is complex, manual, and high-stakes.

Most time and cost comes from administrative preparation: collecting documents, extracting information, checking consistency, assembling evidence, and rework when issues are found late.

Applicants

Overwhelming paperwork, repetitive data entry, high error risk.

Law firms

Admin-heavy workflows under fixed-fee pressure; scaling often means hiring more people.

Employers & universities

Time-consuming processes, compliance risk, and peak-season bottlenecks.

Why “generic AI tools” don’t fit regulated work

No auditability, no case-level controls, and unclear boundaries that can blur accountability.

Where risk concentrates today

Fragmented channels + manual re-entry means inconsistencies are often discovered late, under deadline pressure.

How Visagen works

Same actors. Same accountability. Better infrastructure.

Visagen introduces structure and validation at the start of preparation, then generates drafts for humans to review and approve.

01
Upload documents into a structured case workspace

Replace scattered email/portal/message uploads with one case context.

02
Extract, normalise, and validate information

Identify gaps and internal inconsistencies early — before the deadline crunch.

03
Generate draft forms, checklists, and evidence packs

Draft materials are prepared for review, not submission.

04
Caseworker edits. Supervisor signs off. Submission happens externally.

Human judgement stays central; Visagen strengthens preparation and auditability.

Visagen is a preparation + validation layer, not a decision-maker — it sits between documents and professionals, not between professionals and judgement.
Product

Four-layer architecture designed for repeatability and auditability.

The core system focuses on defensible automation: document handling, extraction, schema-based rules, and pack generation.

Document processing

OCR, auto-splitting, classification, and quality checks.

AI extract + reasoning

Identity extraction, history parsing, cross-checking, and gap detection.

Visa logic engine

Schema-based rules, dynamic forms, multi-route scalability, compliance triggers.

Pack generator

Draft forms, cover letters, checklists, and evidence organisation for review.

Clear boundaries

AI supports preparation tasks; it does not provide advice, determine eligibility, or make decisions.

Designed for audit readiness

Human approval, explainable outputs, and a workflow designed for oversight and defensibility.

Data protection, cybersecurity, and trust

Designed with UK GDPR / DPA 2018 principles in mind: case-level data isolation, role-based access, and audit logging. Client data is not used to train models and is processed only for the relevant matter. Controls align with UK standards such as Cyber Essentials and ISO 27001 principles (least privilege, secure handling, separated environments).
